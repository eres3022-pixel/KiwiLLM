KiwiLLM Together — Program Rules

Overview

Welcome to KiwiLLM Together — our referral and reward program that lets you earn real API Credits simply by inviting friends to the platform. Please read these rules carefully. By participating, you confirm you have read and agree to them in full.

  • Program Name: KiwiLLM Together
  • Platform: Web dashboard at kiwillm.in — accessible from any browser on any device, worldwide.
  • Period: Ongoing. KiwiLLM reserves the right to pause, adjust, or terminate the program with prior notice.
  • Region: Available globally. Availability in certain regions may be limited by payment capabilities or local policy.
  • Eligible Participants: Any registered KiwiLLM user with a valid individual account. Enterprise workspace owners may act as inviters but are not eligible to receive draw chances as invitees.

I. How to Participate & Earn Draw Chances

1. Eligibility
All registered KiwiLLM users may participate. You must use your own account. Participating through another person's account or impersonating another user is strictly prohibited.

2. Task Types & Draw Chances
Complete either task below. Both the inviter and the invitee receive 1 draw chance upon successful completion.

TaskHow It Works
Invite a friend to join KiwiLLMYour friend signs up via your unique invite link and generates their first API key within 24 hours of registration.
Invite a friend to top up creditsYour friend completes their first credit top-up on KiwiLLM within 24 hours of clicking your invite link.

⚠ If the invitee does not complete the required action within the time limit, the invitation is void and no draw chances are granted to either party.

3. How to Invite
Visit the Invite page on your KiwiLLM dashboard to generate your unique referral link. Share it with friends. When they click your link, sign up, and complete a qualifying task, the invitation is confirmed automatically.

4. Invitation Rules

  • Only the first successful invite from any given person counts. Duplicate invitations from the same user do not earn additional draws.
  • "Same user" is determined by account, email, payment method, or other reasonable means. KiwiLLM reserves the right to make this determination.
  • You must participate personally. Using bots, scripts, bulk accounts, or false identities to generate invites is strictly prohibited.

5. Draw Mechanic
Click Draw Now on this page to spend 1 draw chance. The wheel spins and awards a random prize. Draw chances are non-refundable once consumed.

III. Credit Distribution & Usage

  • Instant delivery: Credits are added to your balance immediately after winning. Brief delays may occur due to network conditions.
  • Usage: Credits work like any other KiwiLLM balance — use them across all supported models at api.kiwillm.in.
  • Expiry priority: If you have multiple credit sources, credits with the earliest expiry date are consumed first.
  • Non-transferable: Prize credits may not be transferred, gifted, or sold to other users in any form.

IV. Cancellation & Anti-Fraud

  • If an invitee's top-up is refunded or reversed, the associated task is deemed incomplete. Unused draw chances tied to that task will be reclaimed; prizes already drawn remain unaffected.
  • Fraud, automation, fake accounts, coordinated abuse, or any attempt to manipulate the program will result in immediate disqualification, revocation of all earned rewards, and potential permanent account suspension.
  • KiwiLLM may suspend or terminate the program at any time due to technical issues, regulatory requirements, or force majeure. Advance notice will be provided wherever possible.

V. General

KiwiLLM reserves the right to amend these rules at any time. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued participation after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised rules. For support, contact us via the KiwiLLM dashboard or join our community on Telegram and Discord.
